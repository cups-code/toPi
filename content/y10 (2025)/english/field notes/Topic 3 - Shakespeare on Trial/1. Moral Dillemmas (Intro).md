## Defining Justice
- What is justice?
	- Justice is about the fair trial to decide whether someone should be punished or relieved, utilising a set of morals or laws. It is the assessment of actions and consequences.
- Is justice the same as fairness?
	- Justice is not the same as fairness. Fairness is not always related to justice because justice is not always fair.
- Is justice about following rules, or doing what's right?
	- Justice is about following rules because other people's morals and perspectives are not always included in justice.
