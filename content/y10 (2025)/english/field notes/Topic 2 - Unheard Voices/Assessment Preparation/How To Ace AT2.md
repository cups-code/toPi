# Tips to Ace AT2
- Emulate Steinbeck's Style
	- Accent
	- Removing End of Verbs
	- Imagery
		- Does not have to be perfect imagery, must be dependent on the character you choose (e.g. it is not realistic that Lennie will have intense imagery)
		- Use imagery such as auditory, olfactory, gustatory, visual, and tactile imagery
			- This type of vocabulary will assist you in Year 11 and 12
	- Symbolism
		- For example ...
			- Lennie is portrayed as a dog
			- Candy's dog is shot in the field
			- George shoots Lennie near the river
- Understand Characterisation
	- Understand the character and their voice
- Portray Marginalisation
	- Effectively portray how characters are marginalised (without being explicit)
- Allude to Scene
