# Finding Unknown Angles
## Learning Intention
- I can use trigonometry to find the value of an angle in a right-angled triangle.
## Formulae
- Follow [[L01 - Trigonometric Ratios#Formula|SOHCAHTOA]] in terms of the ratios for the angles.

> [!info] Inverse SOHCAHTOA
> $$\theta = \sin^{-1}\left( \frac{O}{H} \right )$$
> $$\theta = \cos^{^-1}\left( \frac{A}{H} \right)$$
> $$\theta = \tan^{-1}\left( \frac{O}{A} \right)$$

- These are the inverse [[L10 - Trigonometric Functions#Sine Function|sine function]], [[L10 - Trigonometric Functions#Cosine Function|cosine function]], and tangent function.
