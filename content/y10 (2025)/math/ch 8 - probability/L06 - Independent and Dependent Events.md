# Independent and Dependent Events
## Learning Intention
- I can check for independent events.
## Independent Events
- Events are independent when the outcome of one event **does not** change the probability of obtaining the other events.
- Let's take the example of two events.

> [!Example] Two Independent Events (Formulae)
> When two events are independent, then the following are true:
> - $P(A|B) = P(A)$
> - $P(B|A) = P(B)$
> - $P(A \cap B) = P(A) \times P(B)$

## Dependent Events
- Dependents events occur when the probability of one outcome **does change** the probability of obtaining the other events.
## Multi-Stage Experiment
- Multi-stage experiments such as two-step experiments have certain conditions.
- If the experiment is **with replacement**, then the successive events are **independent**.
- If the experiment is **without replacement**, then the success events are dependent.
