# Trigonometric Ratios
## Learning Intentions
- I can use trigonometry to solve for a side length in the numerator.
- I can use trigonometry to solve for a side length in the denominator.
## Formula
> [!Info] SOHCAHTOA
> $$S\frac{O}{H}C\frac{A}{H}T\frac{O}{A}$$
> $$sin \theta = \frac{O}{H}$$
> $$cos \theta = \frac{A}{H}$$
> $$tan \theta = \frac{O}{A}$$
- SOHCAHTOA is the acronym for finding the trigonometric ratios.
