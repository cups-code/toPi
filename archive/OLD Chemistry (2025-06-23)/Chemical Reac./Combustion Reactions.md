# Combustion Reactions
*This lesson was not comprehensive enough. Requires further review.*

> [!tip] Additional Info
> This was completed during the same lesson as [[Acidic Reactions]].

- When things burn, they react with oxygen in the air and emit light and heat.
- For combustion to occur, these factors are required.
	- Oxygen
	- Heat
	- Fuel
- Additionally, it is important to remember this word equation for carbon dioxide.
	- Carbon + Oxygen Gas -> Carbon Dioxide
	- This is the same as: $C + O_2 \rightarrow CO_2$
