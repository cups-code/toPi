# Acidic Reactions
- There are some key acidic reactions to remember.

## Metal + Acid Reaction
- An important reaction to remember is defined by this word equation...
	- Metal + Acid -> Salt + Hydrogen

> [!info] Example of Application
> $2Na + 2(HCl) \rightarrow 2(NaCl) + H_2$
> - In words, this is basically: Sodium + Hydrochloric Acid -> Sodium Chloride + Hydrogen Gas
> - This is also an example of a balanced equation. Balanced equations are not covered in Year 10.
## Carbonate + Acid Reaction
- An important reaction to remember is defined by this word equation...
	- Carbonate + Acid -> Salt + Water + Carbon Dioxide

> [!info] Example of Application
> $2(HCl) + Na_2CO_3 \rightarrow 2(NaCl) + H_2O + CO_2$
> - In words, this is basically: Hydrochloric Acid + Sodium Carbonate -> Sodium Chloride + Water + Carbon Dioxide
